About Us

The Open Internship Movement

Every single day on LinkedIn, thousands of Computer Science students drop their emails in the comments of fake internship posts. They are met with professional-looking offer letters, generic copy-pasted projects, and an eventual demand to pay anywhere from ₹199 to ₹999 for a "documentation fee" or a PDF certificate.

We call it what it is: an organized scam business exploiting desperation.

We exist to make internship scams completely irrelevant by giving every capable CS student access to genuine, guided work experience completely free. We don't believe in hoarding knowledge, and we don't believe in charging students to learn.

For Batch 1, our interns aren't building throwaway calculator apps. They are building the infrastructure of this very platform—from the landing page to our automated verification engines. We are a lean, student-led collective proving that high-quality tech experience requires zero financial exploitation.
