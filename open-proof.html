Open Proof System

Code Speaks Louder Than Certificates

The tech industry does not care about fancy border designs on a PDF; it cares about what you can build. Our Open Proof System bridges the gap between traditional college requirements and real-world employment criteria.

How It Works:

  1. Unique Certificate ID: Upon rigorous completion of your project, you are issued a unique tracking ID (e.g., UDX-001).
  2. Live Fetching: Anyone—whether it is your college department head or a future hiring manager—can enter that ID on our verification page.
  3. The Truth Engine: The page dynamically pulls your record directly from our live database. It instantly displays:
    • Your full student profile.
    • Your dedicated track and mentor details.
    • The Proof: A direct hyperlink to your public production commits inside our GitHub Organization.

If your code isn't live in our repository, the certificate doesn't verify. It is simple, un-fakable proof of capability.
