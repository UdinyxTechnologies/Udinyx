Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

help Is this program really completely free?
Yes, always. We do not charge for applications, certificates, or mentorship.
help Do Batch 1 interns get a stipend?
No. Because we are in our early testing phase and do not charge students, Batch 1 is an unpaid, portfolio-building cohort. However, completing Batch 1 gives you exclusive, priority placement for upcoming paid startup roles through our platform as we scale.
help How is this different from open-source programs or other platforms?
Standard open-source programs can be incredibly complex and offer no direct, structured guidance. We provide a structured team environment, a dedicated mentor for every 10 students, clear weekly deadlines, and a direct path to future startup placement.

For Applicants & Interns

help What is the duration and weekly time commitment?
The program runs for 4 to 6 weeks. You must follow our weekly operational rhythm: tasks are assigned on Monday, a mid-week sync happens on Wednesday, code submissions drop on Friday, and mentor reviews are completed by Sunday.
help What tools do I need to participate?
You only need a laptop, an internet connection, a GitHub profile, a Discord account for team communication, and a Notion workspace account for tracking your weekly deliverables.
help Will my college accept this certificate?
Yes. Because our platform is legally registered and provides transparent, live verification linking straight to your actual project output, colleges easily accept our documentation for degree completion requirements.
